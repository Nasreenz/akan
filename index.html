AKAN NAMES

Africa is the home to a variety of people with fasinating different aspects of life.for instance,Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture.
Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are Ghanian day names.

The following are Ghanian names and the days they represent:

  • Saturday-named Ama
