Akan Names

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

The following are some Ghanaian Names:

Female

  • Sunday - Akosua
  • Monday - Adwoa
  • Tuesday - Abenaa
  • Wednesday - Akua
  • Thursday - Yaa
  • Friday - Afua
  • Saturday - Ama

Male

  • Sunday - kwasi
  • Monday - Kwadwo
  • Tuesday - Kwabena
  • Wednesday - Kwaku
  • Thursday - Yaw
  • Friday - Kofi
  • Saturday - Kwame
